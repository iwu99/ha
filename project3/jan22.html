Clouds are like marshmallows under me. I suppose I’m on a really tall mountain. But my situation seems really dangerous because the ground under me no more than 10 square feet. I have no idea why I’m here and how do I get here. But I don’t feel cold as if I was there 1000 years. Suddenly, a girl driving a turtle come to me. She does not talk about took away several chestnut from my head?? The image changes I fall down to the cloud bed. They are like real bed and I can find chestnut inside them. I packed them to a several packages because I want to eat them later. I walked down the mountain, find several people are running up. I asked them what are they chasing, they answered:” the one who stole 5000 chestnuts. That might be me but I said that is a girl with a turtle. Then they becomes that girl and start chasing me. I’m running down the hill but accidently fall into the sea of clouds and I woke up.
