Cannot identify where is it. Probably some kind of old house. But I'm sure there are more than two floors. I got out the room and met a lot of familiar people as if we are in a dorm. I guess I was just wandering around or try to find a exit. The door structure of this building is bizzare. They are like curtains open from low to high. Then, finally arrive at a big open space. To the end of my sight, there is a window and that is opened. Tons of newspapers are blowed into the window toward me. A person standing in front of the window, who is facing me but I cannot see its face.
Suddenly, a sound of a eagle comes, it flys toward that person and bite at its left eyes. When that person opens its eye again, I can see a yellow dot on its eye lid.
